About Us

Welcome to Dewarshi Farm, a heaven of organic farming nestled in the lush landscapes of Nepal. We specialize in growing coffee, oranges, and a variety of fruits, while also practicing sustainable animal husbandry. Our farmstay offers a unique opportunity to connect with nature and experience the joys of rural life.
